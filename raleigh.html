Raleigh

About Raleigh

Raleigh is the capital of North Carolina. Raleigh is an urban city located in Wake County in the central region of North Carolina. According to the 2020 U.S. Census, Raleigh has the second-highest population among North Carolina cities. The estimated median household income in Raleigh is $78,631, according to https://www.census.gov/quickfacts/fact/table/raleighcitynorthcarolina/PST045223. This is higher than the state's $66,186 estimated median household income, according to https://www.census.gov/quickfacts/fact/table/NC/POP010210.

Things to do in Raleigh

There are many fun things to do in Raleigh. Here is a list of some activities to do while in Raleigh.

  • Visit the North Carolina Museum of Natural Sciences.
  • Spend some time at Pullen Park, the oldest public park in North Carolina.
  • Attend an event at PNC Arena.

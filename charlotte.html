Charlotte

About Charlotte

Charlotte is an urban city located in Mecklenburg County in the south-central region of North Carolina. According to the 2020 U.S. Census, Charlotte has the highest population among North Carolina cities. The estimated median household income in Charlotte is $74,070, according to https://www.census.gov/quickfacts/fact/table/charlottecitynorthcarolina/POP060210. This is higher than the state's $66,186 estimated median household income, according to https://www.census.gov/quickfacts/fact/table/NC/POP010210.

Things to do in Charlotte

There are many fun things to do in Charlotte. Here is a list of some activities to do while in Charlotte.

  • Visit the NASCAR Hall of Fame.
  • Ride rollercoasters at Carowinds.
  • Visit the Discovery Place Science Museum.

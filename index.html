About North Carolina

North Carolina is a state located in the southeastern region of the United States of America. North Carolina has a diverse geography that includes mountains and beaches. The state has a population of over ten million. The three counties in North Carolina with the highest population according to the 2020 U.S. Census are:

  1. Wake County
  2. Mecklenburg County
  3. Guilford County

The following table shows the three cities in North Carolina with the highest population.

North Carolina cities with the highest population, according to the 2020 U.S. Census
City Population County
Charlotte 874,579 Mecklenburg
Raleigh 467,665 Wake
Greensboro 299,035 Guilford

Quick Facts

  • State Capital: Raleigh
  • State Nickname: The Tar Heel State
  • State Flower: Flowering Dogwood
  • State Bird: Red Cardinal

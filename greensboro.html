Greensboro

About Greensboro

Greensboro is an urban city located in Guilford County in the north-central region of North Carolina. According to the 2020 U.S. Census, Greensboro has the third highest population among North Carolina cities. The estimated median household income in Greensboro is $55,051, according to https://www.census.gov/quickfacts/fact/table/greensborocitynorthcarolina/INC110222. This is lower than the state's $66,186 estimated median household income, according to https://www.census.gov/quickfacts/fact/table/NC/POP010210.

Things to do in Greensboro

There are many fun things to do in Greensboro. Here is a list of some activities to do while in Greensboro.

  • Spend time at the the Bog Garden at Benjamin Park.
  • Visit the Weatherspoon Art Museum.
  • Visit the International Civil Rights Center & Museum.
